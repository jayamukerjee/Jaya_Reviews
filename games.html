Since I’ve never actually owned a game console or anything to play games I was interested in, the only times I would play Mario Kart was when I was at my family friends houses’ mostly when I was younger. And I loved it. Maybe because I played it so sparingly, so I never got bored of it, but I also just loved racing the different character and choosing different cart parts to make a cool or cute combo. I think when I do get a Switch and play it more often the flashiness will wear off, but it will always be one of my favourite games.
